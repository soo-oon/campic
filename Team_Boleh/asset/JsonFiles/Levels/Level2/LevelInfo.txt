Player Information:
	- Normal Size
	- Located Left

Clear Condition:
	- Player need to jump over the pit - if fell into the pit, cannot climb back up
	
	Option1- Use Round-Trip Platform moving left and right to cross the pit (Moving only left-half side)
	Option2- Capture player itself to make platform to cross the pit

Objective:
	- It is physically impossible to jump over the pit without camera feature. Therefore must use it at least once.
	- Teach players basic feature of camera (can change dynamic object to static)
	- Number of film decreases corresponding to the number of capture - Tells capture has limit
