Player Information:
	- Normal Size
	- Located Left
	- No Camera

Clear Condition:
	- Player need to jump over the pit - if fell into the pit, still able to climb up

Objective:
	- Teach players basic control of movement
	- Left side is blocked therefore only way to move is to the right
	- Teach players how to jump and how much they can jump
	- Right side is also blocked, and before the wall, there is portal to the next level
	  Because it is the only object to interact with, player have no other option than
	  to make contact with the portal
